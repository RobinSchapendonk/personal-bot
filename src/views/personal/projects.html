PokeHunt

PokeHunt is a Pokémon Discord bot that lets players catch, collect, trade & battle while completing various quests. In an active channel, the bot will randomly spawn a Pokémon, which you can catch. After you've caught it, you can level it up, or sell it on the market. You can also battle with other players, and see who has the strongest Pokémon!
